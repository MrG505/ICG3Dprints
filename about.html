About ICG3Dprints

ICG3Dprints delivers premium, high-detail 3D printed creations using a Bambu Lab P1P for exceptional speed, precision, and reliability.

From functional gadgets and Mustang scale models to Star Wars collectibles, fantasy pieces like the Dragon Skull Cat Bed, and custom signs — we turn ideas into reality with top-tier PLA, PETG, and other filaments.

We offer both physical prints (carefully packaged and shipped) and instant digital STL files for makers who want to print themselves.
